Block Management

From day-to-day management to advising developers on the design of communal areas, we take care of anything and everything relating to apartment blocks and their communal areas.

We work closely with developers to ensure that everything, from lifts to gardens, are designed so that they can be managed practically and cost effectively. We then offer advice and assistance to set service charge budgets, provide pre-completion consultancy to the house builders, and carry out consultations for major works and long-term agreements.

Advice and assistance for achieving practical and cost-effective management

Our clients range from investor funds to private landlords and resident management companies, with properties across the spectrum, from 500-unit new-builds to converted blocks in prime estates.

We deal with the tendering and management of maintenance contracts, and the appointment and management of onsite staff to ensure the smooth running of the building. The team has a wealth of experience managing a variety of residential assets for a multitude of clients, so we are often called for expert witness and mediation.
